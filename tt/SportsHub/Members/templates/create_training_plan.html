Instructions for creating a training plan:

  • Consider the user's budget and set a reasonable price limit for the plan.
  • Ensure user comfortability by tailoring the plan to their fitness level and goals.
  • Take into account any gym-related conditions or restrictions when designing the workouts.
  • Balance cardiovascular, strength, and flexibility exercises to create a well-rounded plan.
  • Provide clear instructions for each exercise, including proper form and technique.
  • Consider any medical conditions or injuries the user may have and adapt the plan accordingly.
  • Ensure that the plan aligns with the user's specific fitness goals, whether it's weight loss, muscle gain, or overall fitness.
  • Offer variations and modifications for exercises to accommodate users of different skill levels.
